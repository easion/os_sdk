News

A new experimental version, Netbas os 0.30 has been released (screenshot). This version includes the following features: realization of a simple posix thread library, xynth were ported and become a part of its desktop environment, graphical browser links 2.30, bash 2.05a, quake, sdl, ftp server, as well as some simple unix command packet this release, and its livedemo can be downloaded from the following address.

http://jicama.cosoft.org.cn/downloads/livecd.zip
